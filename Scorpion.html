Scorpion is an American drama television series loosely based on the life of self-proclaimed genius and computer expert Walter O'Brien.[1] The series follows Walter O'Brien and his team of genius outcasts as they are recruited by federal agent Cabe Gallo of the U.S. Department of Homeland Security to form Scorpion, said to be the last line of defense against complex, high-tech threats around the globe.

The series premiered on September 22, 2014.[2] In March 2016, the series was renewed for a third season, which is set to premiere on October 3, 2016.[3][4] As of April 25, 2016[update], 46 episodes of Scorpion have aired, concluding the second season.
