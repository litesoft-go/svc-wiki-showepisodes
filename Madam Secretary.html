List of Madam Secretary episodes

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Madam Secretary is an American political drama television series created by Barbara Hall and executive produced by Lori McCreary and Morgan Freeman. It stars Téa Leoni as Elizabeth "Bess" Adams McCord, a CIA analyst who is promoted to United States Secretary of State. On March 25, 2016, CBS renewed the show for a third season, which is set to premiere on October 2, 2016.[1][2]

As of May 8, 2016[update], 45 episodes of Madam Secretary have aired, concluding the second season.
