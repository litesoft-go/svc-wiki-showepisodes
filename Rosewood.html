Rosewood is an American police procedural television series created by Todd Harthan, starring Morris Chestnut as Dr. Beaumont Rosewood Jr., a private pathologist working in Miami, Florida, in high demand with law enforcement.[1] The series premiered on Fox on September 23, 2015.[2][3] On October 16, 2015, Fox picked up Rosewood for a full season of 22 episodes.[4] On April 7, 2016 Fox renewed the series for a second season.

Meanwhile, Hornstock enlists the help of Villa as he attempts to reconnect with one of the witnesses linked to the case, his estranged teenage daughter.

Reception[edit]

Rosewood has been met with generally negative reviews from critics. On Rotten Tomatoes, season 1 has a rating of 7%, based on 44 reviews, with an average rating of 3.5/10.[43] Metacritic gave season one of the show a score of 37 out of 100, based on 23 reviews, signifying "generally unfavorable reviews".[44]
