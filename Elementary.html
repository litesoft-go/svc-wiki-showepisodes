Elementary is an American crime drama created by Robert Doherty and loosely based on Sherlock Holmes and other characters appearing in the works of Sir Arthur Conan Doyle. The series stars Jonny Lee Miller, Lucy Liu, Aidan Quinn, and Jon Michael Hill and premiered on CBS on September 27, 2012. On May 11, 2015, the series was renewed for a fourth season, which premiered on November 5, 2015.[1] On March 25, 2016, it was renewed for a fifth season, which is set to premiere on October 2, 2016.[2][3]

As of May 8, 2016[update], 96 episodes of Elementary have aired, concluding the fourth season.
